# Image credits

Every image in this directory is either public domain or under a Creative
Commons licence permitting reuse with attribution. Licence, author, and source
are recorded below, and each is credited in the caption where it appears.

No copyrighted figure is reproduced from a paper. Where the course needed a
result from the literature that is not freely licensed — Laughlin's contrast
matching, the Atick–Redlich filter family, the opponent axes — the figure is
**recomputed from the published equations** and labelled as such, rather than
copied.
